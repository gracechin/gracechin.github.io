About

I am a final (fourth) year MEng Design Engineering Imperial College student. This course consists of many project-based assessments, which not only trained me to actively apply a variety of general engineering knowledge, but also developed my team work and communication skills. Having experienced working as an intern in two tech startups (Emotech Ltd. in London and CISC Limited in Hong Kong), I am also aware of the fast-paced intense environment of startups and enjoy the challenge.

I am interested in programming and designing creative solutions to challenges that will make an impactful contribution to society and help people. Through additional experience, I hope to further practice, improve and learn.
