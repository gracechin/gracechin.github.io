About

Grace Chin

I am a final (fourth) year MEng Design Engineering undergraduate Imperial College student. This course consists of many project-based assessments, which not only trained me to actively apply a variety of general engineering knowledge, but also developed my team work and communication skills. Having experienced working as an intern in two tech startups, I am also aware of the fast-paced intense environment of startups and enjoy the challenge.

I am interested in programming and designing creative solutions to challenges that will make an impactful contribution to society and help people. Through additional experience, I hope to further practice, improve and learn.
